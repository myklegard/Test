VA Innovation and Creation Series

Prosthetics and Assistive Technology Challenge

About

Our vision is to accelerate development of personalized technologies to improve care and quality of life for Veterans faced with disabilities by initiating the VA Innovation Creation Series.

Goals

1. Improve patient care and quality of life for our Veterans and their supporters.

2. Create an open ecosystem of designs of prosthetics + assistive technologies.

3. Demonstrate the value of rapid prototyping and co-creation to develop personalized technologies for Veterans and infuse this practice into everyday healthcare practices.

4. Spread awareness of the prosthetics + assistive technologies capabilities of the VA.

The event will launch at the VA Palo Alto Medical Center at 4:30pm. We would love to have you at this free launch party. You can register for the event here.

Also we are holding a series of maker workshops on Saturday, May 16th. If you are interested in attending those workshops, please sign up here (they are free too!).

Timeline

Challenges

Develop novel upper and lower extremity prosthesis terminal devices for daily use.

Create a medication pill box that allows the flexibility to hold medications that need to be taken up to 8 times a day with a reminder system for each time medication needs to be taken.

Create a device that can dampen an essential tremor when a veteran is performing fine motor tasks.

Design a device to remotely change the speed and grip strength of a prosthetic device for our veterans with upper extremity injuries.
